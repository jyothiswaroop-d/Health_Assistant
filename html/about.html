About Hospital Database

The hospital database is a critical component in modern healthcare management. It allows for the efficient management of patient information, medical records, appointment schedules, and billing. This database system is designed to ensure that all hospital data is stored securely, is easily accessible by authorized personnel, and complies with healthcare regulations. Key functionalities include:

  • Patient Information Management
  • Medical History Tracking
  • Appointment Scheduling
  • Billing and Payment Processing
  • Staff and Department Management

The database also integrates with other healthcare systems to improve data flow across departments and provide better patient care.
